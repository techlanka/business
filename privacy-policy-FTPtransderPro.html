Privacy Policy

FTP Transfer Pro respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information, including the use of third-party services such as Google AdMob and Firebase.

Information Collection

FTP Transfer Pro does not directly collect, store, sell, or share personal information through its own servers.

However, the app uses third-party services provided by Google, including Firebase and Google AdMob. These services may collect certain information automatically, such as device information, app usage data, crash or performance data, advertising identifiers, and interaction data, depending on the services enabled in the app.

Network Usage

The app uses your local WiFi network to enable file transfer via FTP. File transfers happen locally between devices connected to the same network.

Data Storage and File Transfers

All files accessed or transferred using FTP Transfer Pro remain on your device or on the local device selected by the user for transfer. The app does not upload, store, or process your transferred files on external servers.

Firebase Services

This app may use Firebase services provided by Google to support app functionality, analytics, authentication, database features, performance monitoring, crash reporting, or service improvements.

Depending on the Firebase services enabled, Firebase may collect information such as:

  • Device information
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Crash reports and diagnostics
  • Authentication-related information, if sign-in features are used
  • Database-related data, if cloud database features are used

Firebase data is handled according to Google's Firebase privacy and security practices. For more information, please refer to:

https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Advertising - Google AdMob

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use information such as device data, advertising ID, app interaction data, and ad performance data to provide, personalize, and measure ads.

Users may be able to reset or delete their advertising ID through their device settings, depending on their Android version and Google Play services settings.

For more information about how Google uses data for advertising, please refer to:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Google Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Permissions

The app uses only the permissions necessary for its functionality, which may include:

  • Network access for FTP connection and local WiFi communication
  • Storage or file access for selecting and transferring files
  • Notifications for background service status
  • Internet access for advertisements and Firebase services

Data Sharing

FTP Transfer Pro does not automatically share your files or personal documents. All file transfers are initiated and controlled by the user over the local network.

Third-party services such as Firebase and Google AdMob may process data as described in their respective privacy policies and service terms.

Security

File transfers occur locally over your WiFi network. The app does not upload transferred files to external servers.

Please note that FTP is a local network transfer method and may not provide encryption by default. Users should only use the app on trusted WiFi networks.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase
  • Google AdMob

These services may collect and process information according to their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

FTP Transfer Pro is not specifically directed toward children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so that appropriate action can be taken.

User Control

Users control which files they select and transfer through the app. Users may also manage app permissions through their Android device settings.
