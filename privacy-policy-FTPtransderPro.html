Privacy Policy

FTP Transfer Pro respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

Information Collection

FTP Transfer Pro does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Network Usage

The app uses your local WiFi network to enable file transfer via FTP. All data transfer happens locally between devices connected to the same network.

Data Storage

All files accessed or transferred remain on your device. The app does not upload, store, or process any files on external servers.

Data Sharing

FTP Transfer Pro does not automatically share any data. All file transfers are initiated and controlled by the user over their local network.

Advertising (AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect non-personal information such as device data, advertising ID, and interaction data.

For more information, please refer to Google's privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Permissions

The app uses only necessary permissions for its functionality:

  • Network access for FTP connection
  • Storage access for file transfer
  • Notifications for background service status

Security

All file transfers occur locally over your WiFi network. No personal data is transmitted externally, ensuring your information remains secure on your device.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google AdMob for advertising. No other third-party services are used to collect personal data.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
