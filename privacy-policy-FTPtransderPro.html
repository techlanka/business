Privacy Policy

FTP Transfer Pro respects your privacy.

FTP Transfer Pro does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data. All file transfers happen locally over your WiFi network. No files or personal information are sent to external servers.

Permissions We Use

The app uses permissions only for the following purposes:

  • File access: To allow users to share and transfer files through the FTP server.
  • Network access: To create a local FTP connection over WiFi.
  • Notifications: To show background FTP server status while the service is running.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information, files, usage data, or device data.

Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties.
