Privacy Policy

LabelPrint Pro respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

Information Collection

LabelPrint Pro does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Camera Permission

The app uses your device camera only for scanning barcodes. No images or data from the camera are stored or transmitted.

Data Storage

All scanned barcodes and related data are stored locally on your device. This data is not shared with any external servers.

Data Sharing

The app allows users to export barcode data as CSV files. This action is fully controlled by the user, and no data is shared automatically.

Third-Party Services

LabelPrint Pro does not use third-party services that collect personal data.

Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, your information remains सुरक्षित on your device.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
