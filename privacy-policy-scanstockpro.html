TechDX / Techlanka ("us", "we", or "our") operates the ScanStock Pro Android application. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use ScanStock Pro. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

ScanStock Pro is designed as a barcode-based stock counting, inventory management, and price checking tool for retail stores, warehouses, supermarkets, and businesses.

By using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection And Use

We collect limited information only as necessary to provide app functionality and improve user experience.

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

ScanStock Pro does not require users to create an online account and does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, address, payment details, or phone number.

Staff Names Entered by Users

The app may allow users to enter staff names for stock counting records, internal reporting, or multi-user counting purposes. This information is stored locally on your device unless you export reports.

Files and Imported Data

The app allows users to import CSV or spreadsheet files containing product information such as:
• Barcode
• Product Name
• Category
• Price
• Stock Quantity
• Unit Information

Imported files are used only inside the app for lookup, counting, and reporting functions.

Camera Permission

ScanStock Pro may request camera permission to scan product barcodes. Camera access is used only for barcode scanning and is not used to record, store, or upload photos or video.

Usage / Diagnostic Data

Limited technical data such as device model, Android version, crash logs, or performance diagnostics may be processed by Android system services for app stability.

How Data is Used

Data handled by the app may be used for:
• Barcode scanning
• Price checking
• Stock counting
• Variance calculation
• Review lists and history reports
• Excel export (.xlsx)
• Improving app performance and fixing issues

Storage of Data

Product files, count history, exported reports, and user settings may be stored locally on your device to support normal app functionality.

We do not upload your inventory files or reports to our servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

ScanStock Pro may use Android system components, barcode scanning libraries, or file handling components necessary for app operation.

If analytics, cloud sync, or advertising services are added in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect locally stored information. However, no electronic storage system is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes become effective when published on this page.
