Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how ScanStock Pro handles information when you use the application.

ScanStock Pro is designed for barcode price checking, inventory lookup, barcode generation, exporting barcode labels, and printing barcode labels.

Information Collection

ScanStock Pro does not collect, store, or share personal information on external servers.

The app may access the following features only to provide app functionality:

  • Camera access for barcode scanning
  • Storage access for importing CSV files and exporting barcode labels
  • Printing services for barcode label printing
  • Internet access for Google AdMob advertisements

CSV Files & Product Data

CSV files imported into the app remain stored locally on your device. Product information is not uploaded to any external server.

Barcode Generator

Barcode labels generated using the app are created locally on the device. Exported barcode images are saved only to locations selected by the user.

Advertising

ScanStock Pro uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect limited device information and advertising identifiers in accordance with Google's policies.

Learn more about Google's privacy practices:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services including:

  • Google ML Kit Barcode Scanner
  • Google AdMob

Data Security

All imported files and generated barcode labels are processed locally on your device. The developer does not access or transmit your inventory data.

Children's Privacy

This application is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
