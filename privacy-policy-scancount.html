Techlanka ("us", "we", or "our") operates the ScanCount Pro Android application.

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

ScanCount Pro is designed as a barcode-based stock counting, inventory management, and price checking tool for retail stores, warehouses, supermarkets, and businesses.

By using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection And Use

We collect limited information only as necessary to provide app functionality and improve user experience.

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

ScanCount Pro does not require users to create an online account and does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, address, payment details, or phone number.

Staff Names Entered by Users

The app may allow users to enter staff names for stock counting records, internal reporting, or multi-user counting purposes. This information is stored locally on your device unless exported by the user.

Files and Imported Data

The app allows users to import CSV/XLSX spreadsheet files containing product information such as:

• Barcode
• Product Name
• Category
• Price
• Stock Quantity
• Unit Information

Imported files are used only inside the app for stock lookup, counting, and reporting functions.

Camera Permission

ScanCount Pro may request camera access to scan product barcodes. Camera access is used only for barcode scanning and is not used to record, store, or upload photos or videos.

Usage Data

Limited technical information such as device type, Android version, crash logs, and diagnostic information may be processed by Android system services, Firebase services, or app frameworks for stability and app improvement purposes.

Use of Data

TechDX / Techlanka uses the handled data for the following purposes:

• Barcode scanning
• Stock counting
• Price checking
• Variance calculations
• Exporting CSV/XLSX reports
• Improving app performance and stability
• Diagnosing and fixing technical issues

Storage Of Data

Imported product files, count history, exported reports, and app settings may be stored locally on your device to support normal app functionality and faster reuse.

We do not upload your inventory files or reports to our servers.
