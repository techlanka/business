Privacy Policy

ScanCount Pro respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

Information Collection

ScanCount Pro does not require users to create an account and does not intentionally collect personal information.

Inventory & File Usage

The app allows users to import CSV/XLSX inventory files containing product information such as barcode, product name, category, price, stock quantity, and unit information.

Imported files are processed locally on the device for barcode lookup, stock counting, inventory management, and report generation.

Camera Usage

ScanCount Pro uses the device camera for barcode scanning functionality. The app does not record, store, or upload photos or videos.

Local Data Storage

Imported inventory files, counting history, exported reports, and app settings may be stored locally on your device to improve app functionality and user experience.

The app does not upload your inventory files or reports to external servers.

Advertising (AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect non-personal information such as device identifiers, advertising ID, diagnostic information, and ad interaction data.

For more information, please refer to Google's privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Firebase Services

ScanCount Pro may use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics for app diagnostics, crash reporting, and performance improvements.

These services may process limited technical information such as device type, Android version, crash logs, and anonymous usage diagnostics.

Permissions

The app uses only necessary permissions for its functionality:

  • Camera access for barcode scanning
  • Storage/file access for CSV/XLSX import and export
  • Internet access for app updates, AdMob, and Firebase services
  • Notifications for app-related services if enabled

Data Sharing

ScanCount Pro does not sell, rent, or share inventory data or personal information with third parties.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect locally stored data. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google Play Services
  • Google Barcode Scanner APIs

Children's Privacy

ScanCount Pro is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
