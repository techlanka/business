ScanStock Pro is designed as a barcode-based inventory and price lookup tool. By using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection And Use

We collect limited information only as needed to provide and improve the Service.

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

ScanStock Pro does not require users to create an account and does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or payment details.

Files and User-Provided Data

The app allows users to import CSV files containing product and inventory data. These files are selected by the user and are used only for product lookup and display inside the app.
Usage Data
When you use the Service, limited technical information may be processed by the Android system or app framework for normal operation, such as device type, operating system version, crash logs, and diagnostic information.
Camera Data
ScanStock Pro may request camera access to scan product barcodes. Camera access is used only for barcode scanning and is not used to record, store, or transmit images or video to our servers.

Use of Data

TechDX uses the data handled by the app for the following purposes: To provide barcode scanning functionality To allow CSV-based inventory lookup To display product information such as item description, prices, category, and stock quantity To improve app stability and user experience To diagnose and fix technical issues

Storage of Data

Imported CSV references and related app data may be stored locally on your device to support app functionality and faster reuse. We do not upload your CSV files or product data to our servers.

Transfer Of Data

ScanStock Pro does not intentionally transfer personal data to external servers as part of its core functionality. If third-party services are added in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
