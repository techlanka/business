# Privacy Policy – ScanCount Pro Effective Date: May 2026 ScanCount Pro (“the App”) is developed to assist users with inventory management, barcode scanning, stock counting, and product price checking. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user data and device permissions. ## Information We Collect ScanCount Pro does not require account registration and does not directly collect personal identity information. However, the App may process the following data locally on your device: * Imported CSV/XLSX inventory files * Product barcode data * Product stock quantities * Staff/operator names entered inside the app * Count history and exported reports All inventory-related data is stored locally on the user's device unless explicitly exported by the user. ## Camera Access The App uses your device camera for barcode scanning functionality. Camera access is used only while scanning product barcodes and is not used for recording videos or capturing photos. ## File Access The App may request access to files and storage in order to: * Import CSV inventory files * Export CSV/XLSX reports * Save inventory sessions locally The App does not upload imported files to external servers. ## Firebase Services ScanCount Pro uses Google Firebase services including: ### Firebase Analytics Used to understand app performance and improve user experience. ### Firebase Crashlytics Used to identify crashes and technical issues for stability improvements. Firebase may collect: * Device information * App performance data * Crash logs * General usage statistics For more information, please review: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy ## Google Play In-App Updates The App may use Google Play In-App Updates to deliver important updates and improvements directly through Google Play services. ## Data Security We take reasonable steps to protect locally stored app data. However, users are responsible for maintaining backups of exported inventory files and reports. ## Third-Party Services The App may use third-party services provided by: * Google Play Services * Firebase Analytics * Firebase Crashlytics * Google Barcode Scanner APIs These services may collect limited technical information according to their own privacy policies. ## Children's Privacy ScanCount Pro is not intended for children under the age of 13. ## Changes to This Privacy Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any future updates will be reflected on this page. ## Contact For questions regarding this Privacy Policy: techlanka.lk
