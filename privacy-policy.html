Techlanka ("us", "we", or "our") operates the ScanCount Pro Android application. We operate few more apps namely LK Updates News Sri Lanka & Tech Tamil too. Below applies to all apps managed by us.
This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.
ScanCount Pro is designed as a barcode-based inventory, stock counting, and price lookup tool. By using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection And Use

We collect limited information only as needed to provide and improve the Service.

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

ScanCount Pro does not require users to create an account and does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or payment details.

Files and User-Provided Data

The app allows users to import CSV/XLSX files containing product and inventory data. These files are selected by the user and are used only for inventory lookup, stock counting, and display inside the app.
Usage Data
When you use the Service, limited technical information may be processed by the Android system, Firebase services, or app framework for normal operation, such as device type, operating system version, crash logs, app diagnostics, and anonymous usage statistics.
Camera Data
ScanCount Pro may request camera access to scan product barcodes. Camera access is used only for barcode scanning and is not used to record, store, or transmit images or video to our servers.

Use of Data

TechDX uses the data handled by the app for the following purposes: To provide barcode scanning functionality To allow CSV/XLSX-based inventory lookup To display product information such as item description, prices, category, and stock quantity To support stock counting and export reports To improve app stability and user experience To diagnose and fix technical issues To provide app updates through Google Play services

Storage of Data

Imported CSV/XLSX references, count history, and related app data may be stored locally on your device to support app functionality and faster reuse. We do not upload your inventory files or product data to our servers.

Transfer Of Data

ScanCount Pro does not intentionally transfer personal inventory data to external servers as part of its core functionality. Some technical diagnostic data may be processed through Firebase services provided by Google.

Disclosure Of Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data.

To protect against legal liability.

Service Providers

ScanCount Pro may use trusted third-party services for app functionality and diagnostics including: Google Play Services Firebase Analytics Firebase Crashlytics Google Barcode Scanner APIs These services may process limited technical information required for app stability, analytics, and barcode scanning functionality.
