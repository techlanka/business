Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

ScanStock Pro respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use it.

1. Information We Collect

ScanStock Pro does not collect, store, or share personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location
  • Payment details

2. Camera Permission

The app may request access to your device camera only for barcode scanning. Camera data is processed on the device and is not uploaded to external servers.

3. Files and CSV Imports

ScanStock Pro allows users to import CSV files for inventory and product lookup.

  • Imported files remain on your device
  • No files are uploaded to external servers
  • The app only accesses files selected by the user

4. Local Data Storage

The app may store certain settings or imported file references locally on your device to improve functionality and user experience. No cloud storage is used unless introduced in a future update.

5. Third-Party Services

Currently, ScanStock Pro does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. If such services are added in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

6. Data Security

We aim to protect your information by processing app-related data locally on your device whenever possible.

7. Children’s Privacy

ScanStock Pro is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
