Privacy Policy

BudgetWise Pro respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your financial information and personal data.

Information Collection

BudgetWise Pro does not collect, store, sell, or transmit your personal financial information to external servers.

All financial records entered into the app, including transactions, budgets, bill reminders, savings goals, settings, and preferences, are stored locally on your device unless you explicitly choose to export or back up your data.

Financial Data

BudgetWise Pro allows users to record financial information such as income, expenses, budgets, bill reminders, and related notes.

This information is stored locally on your device and is used solely to provide budgeting, expense tracking, reporting, and financial management features.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using Android storage mechanisms.

The app does not maintain cloud servers and does not automatically upload your financial information to any external service.

Backup and Restore

BudgetWise Pro includes backup and restore functionality that allows users to export and import their financial data.

Backups are created only when initiated by the user and are stored in a location selected by the user.

The app does not automatically upload backup files to any server.

Security Features

BudgetWise Pro provides optional security features such as PIN protection and biometric authentication (fingerprint, face unlock, or device credentials, depending on device capabilities).

Authentication data used by biometric security is managed by your device operating system and is never accessed, stored, or transmitted by BudgetWise Pro.

Permissions

The app uses only the permissions necessary for its functionality, which may include:

  • Biometric authentication for optional app security
  • Storage or file access for backup and restore operations
  • Notifications for bill reminders and financial alerts

Data Sharing

BudgetWise Pro does not share, sell, rent, or transmit your financial information to third parties.

All financial records remain under the user's control and are stored locally unless the user manually exports a backup file.

Internet Usage

BudgetWise Pro is designed to function without requiring an online account.

The app does not require users to create an account and does not transmit financial records to external servers.

Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect your data through local storage, optional PIN protection, and biometric authentication features.

Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their devices and any backup files they create.

Third-Party Services

BudgetWise Pro does not rely on external cloud services to store financial data.

If future versions of the app introduce optional third-party integrations, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Children's Privacy

BudgetWise Pro is not specifically directed toward children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so that appropriate action can be taken.

User Control

Users have full control over their financial records stored within the app.

Users may edit, delete, export, back up, restore, or clear their data at any time using the app's built-in features.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
